Clash Lane

This side lane is for durable Fighters or Tanks who excel in 1v1 battles and pushing towers.

Jungle Lane

Home to Assassins or Fighters who focus on invading the enemy jungle, ganking lanes, and securing objectives.

Mid Lane

The strategic center, ideal for Mages or Assassins with high burst damage and map control.

Farm Lane

This side lane prioritizes Marksmen who focus on farming minions efficiently to become the team's late-game carry.

Roamer

Usually Tanks or Supports who excel at absorbing damage, setting up kills for teammates, and providing utility like heals or shields.
